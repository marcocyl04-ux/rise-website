Enhanced Ice Rink
What's Different
The same Three.js ice rink foundation, enhanced with real hockey elements. Move your mouse over the hero to control the camera.
  • Hockey pucks — 3D puck objects that glide and tumble across the ice surface
  • Skate trails — ice shaving particles that spawn behind moving pucks
  • Depth fog — atmospheric fade so the far end of the rink recedes naturally
  • Better rink detail — face-off dots, goal creases, blue line fills, boards with height
  • Ice reflections — subtle reflective floor that catches light from the pucks
  • Board glow — faint red edge lighting on the boards for depth
